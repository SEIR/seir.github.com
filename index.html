Swÿp Hack Night @Intrepid

When:
Saturday, February 18th from 6:30PM—2:00AM.
Who:
Any MIT student who likes to hack on software.
What?
Our theme is 'expression;' you, assisted by the likes of local devs, will hack on a application that best expresses something.
By 1:30AM, your app will hopefully win the cheering, geering, laughing support of your peers, and thus the startup gift basket containing various APIs, magic sample code, or promos from attending developers.
But wait, there's more: every two hours we'll release an additional feature that your app needs to contain, and at 11pm, there'll be lots of food.

It's bound to be a lot of fun, and your pull request and introduction within the commit log will get us to add you to the list! We'll email via your github email :-)
