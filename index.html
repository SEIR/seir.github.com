Swÿp Hack Night @Intrepid

When:
Saturday, February 18th from 6:30PM—2:00AM.
Who:
Any MIT student who likes to hack on software. Local devs who want to join the fun.
What?

Our theme is expression; you, assisted by the likes of local devs, will hack on an application that best expresses something.

By 1:30AM, your app will hopefully win the cheering, geering, laughing support of your peers, and thus the startup gift basket containing various APIs, magic sample code, or promos from attending developers.

Every two hours we'll announce an additional feature that your app should incorporate, and at 11pm, there'll be lots of food.
